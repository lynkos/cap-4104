    Persona 1: Elizabeth



    Age: 24

    Location: Miami, Florida

    Major: Marine Biology

    Technical Comfort: Advanced

    Profession: Student

    Back Story

    • Full-time student balancing coursework, part-time job, and extracurriculars.
    • Struggles with time management and deadlines.
    • Likes using digital tools to organize assignments.
    • Often procrastinates, leading to last-minute stress.

    Motivations

    • Wants to complete assignments before the deadline.
    • Tools to minimize distractions and improve focus.
    • Desire to improve grades.
    • Values user-friendly study platforms.

    Frustrations

    • Easily distracted by social media.
    • Struggles with prioritizing tasks effectively.
    • Finds some time management tools overwhelming.
    • Dislikes cluttered or poorly designed study areas.

    Ideal Experience

    • Tool that encourages users to continue with assignments.
    • Platform that blocks distractions during study sessions.
    • Appreciates websites with cozy designs, enhancing focus.
    • Platform offers personalized study tips and techniques.
    "I often get overwhelmed by deadlines and procrastinate. A tool that helps me stay focused would really help!"
